About Us

Mission

Our story

Provide a secure platform to find verified student roommates at NYU

When we were freshmen at NYU, we faced a problem nearly every NYU student will tell you they have as well- the difficulty in building community, especially in a city school with no central campus. A big consequence of that which we faced first hand is the difficulty in finding compatible roommates.



If for whatever reason your living situation did not align with the people in your immediate friend group- that meant turning to finding roommates on Facebook groups that are prone to scams, hours spent on roommate websites that did not cater to students and difficulty determining roommate compatibility.

The more people we spoke to the clearer it became that not only was this our shared experience, it was the collective experience of nearly every student around us. We decided it as time to build a solution for the future generation of students: a platform where college students could find and interact with their future roommate exclusively using filters and lifestyle questions relevant to college students. Avoiding scams, a safe environment exclusive to students, top matches provided based on compatibility.

Campus Friends: a solution for the future generation of students (and ourselves!)
