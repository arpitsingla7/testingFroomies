Mission

Our story

Provide a secure platform to find verified student roommates at NYU

Finding a roommate for college students has never been easy. Consider the ridiculous hours put into scanning Facebook groups to avoid scams (link to stats) and finding that perfect roommate that aligns with your price range and location. Despite all these obstacles, the roommate experience remains one of the most crucial elements of college life.

A great roommate experience can change the social fabric of time in university, support professional growth and change world views by contributing to daily improvement.

With all roommate finding websites catering to professionals, we want to provide college students a safe space to have the best roommate experience possible without having to worry about verifications and difficulty in search. With filters relevant to college life, Froomies provide a solution to college students looking to room with other students from their university as quickly and efficiently as possible.
